Traditional Medicines & Their Usages

Traditional medicine systems such as Ayurveda, Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), and Homeopathy have been practiced for centuries. These systems focus on holistic healing, using natural herbs, minerals, and therapies to treat various ailments.

  • Ayurveda: Uses herbal remedies, yoga, and dietary guidelines to balance body energies. Common herbs include ashwagandha, turmeric, and tulsi.
  • Traditional Chinese Medicine: Focuses on acupuncture, herbal medicine, and tai chi. Popular herbs include ginseng, gingko biloba, and goji berries.
